Traffic & Misdemeanor Warrants

THERE IS NO ARREST OR BOOKING TO CLEAR THIS WARRANT

If you have an active Misdemeanor or Traffic warrant in Palm Beach County, Florida, Barbie's Bail Bonds can set up a Walkthrough Bond where no booking or arrest is required. The entire process usually lasts about 15-20 minutes where you will remain with our agent the entire time. You will never be left alone.

Walkthrough Bail Bond Process:

  1. Call us at 561-247-0018 to verify the warrant information
  2. Complete our Online Bail Bonds Application - we'll guide you through this
  3. Schedule to meet one of our expert bail bond agents at the Release Lobby of the Palm Beach County Jail
  4. At the jail, the Bail Bond documents will be turned directly to the release officer who will give you a new court date that must be signed for
  5. Sign for your new court date (The only reason you're required to be there is simply to sign for the new court date)
  6. Receive your court date copy - The court date is set for 3 to 4 weeks out
  7. You are free to go! The warrant is cleared from the system and court date is scheduled

Felony Warrants

Felony warrants require booking but by scheduling it with our agents and allowing us to coordinate with the jail, it's the fastest way to get out. All jails are different but Palm Beach County jail usually has you booked within a couple of hours.

We have developed a process we call a "Speedy Booking" where we set up the entire bail bonds process before you ever step foot into the jail ensuring you are in-and-out as quickly as possible.
