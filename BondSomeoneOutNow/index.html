Quick Answers

Common questions about the bail bond process

Most releases happen within 3-6 hours after we receive your information and payment. The exact time depends on the jail's current processing load and the time of day.
You don't pay the full bail amount. You typically pay us 10% of the total bail amount as our fee. We post the full bail with the court. We also offer payment plans to make it even more affordable.
The fee you pay to us (typically 10%) is non-refundable - this is how bail bond companies work. However, if the defendant appears for all court dates, you have no additional obligations to us.
If the defendant misses court, we'll work with you to resolve the situation quickly. We have 180 days to return them to custody. Our priority is helping families stay together, so we'll explore all options before taking enforcement action.
